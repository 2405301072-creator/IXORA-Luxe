Our Story

Where Luxury Meets Responsibility

IXORA was born from a simple belief: that luxury and sustainability are not opposing forces, but kindred spirits. Named after the delicate ixora flower — a symbol of passion, beauty, and connection to the earth — our brand weaves these values into every thread.

Founded by designer Ananya Sharma, IXORA brings together the finest organic cottons, Tencel™ lyocell, and heritage hand-woven linens with contemporary silhouettes that empower the modern woman. Every piece is crafted in our solar-powered atelier by artisans who are fairly paid and passionately committed to their craft.

Our Vision: To redefine luxury fashion as a force for positive change, proving that what we wear can be both breathtakingly beautiful and ethically responsible.
